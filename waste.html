Construction Waste Management

Track, reduce, and manage construction waste the Simplicity way

Simplicity Waste Benchmark

Simplicity uses a unique benchmarking approach to visualize construction waste impact. We calculate the volume of waste generated and divide it by the ground floor area to determine how high the waste would stack if placed on the building footprint.

Lower is better! Our goal is to keep waste height below 100mm, demonstrating exceptional resource efficiency and waste minimization strategies.
