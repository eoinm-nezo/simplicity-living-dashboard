About the Scoring System

Aligned to the leading green-building rating, we actively benchmark the seven credits that matter most — Energy Use, Indoor Environment Quality (winter & summer comfort, moisture), Embodied Carbon, Sustainable Materials, Water Use, Construction Waste Minimisation and Sustainable Transport. Each is scored out of 20 and stands on its own merit, so strengths and weaknesses stay visible instead of averaging into a single star rating.

The score wheel below shows all seven in a single graph: each segment fills from the centre to the score achieved out of 20. Switch to Detailed Results to expand any category and see exactly what it's scored on and why. The 14 remaining rating credits — where Simplicity hits full marks by default — are shown further down the page.
