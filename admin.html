Scoring Assessments

Each row is one assessment. Category scores are out of 20; the combined total is calculated automatically. The Scoring page shows the latest assessment per project.

Embodied Carbon — Life Cycle Modules (kgCO2e/m² GFA)

Drives the Life Cycle Module Breakdown and Carbon Reduction Progress charts on the Embodied Carbon page. Module D (Beyond Life) is usually negative — it's a credit.

Energy Simulation Results

Enter the end-use breakdown from your Nezo OpenStudio report (kWh/m²/year), the annual energy cost ($/m²/year) and a relative initial-cost index (baseline = 100). Group scenario rows feed the methodology benchmark; project rows feed the Simplicity-projects comparison. Tick highlight for the row drawn in Simplicity orange.

Waste Results

Waste height = volume ÷ floor area × 1000 (mm). Under 50mm rates Excellent on the Waste page.

Database Management

The database lives in this browser (localStorage). Export it as a portable .sqlite file to back up to the Drive or share; import to restore. Reset rebuilds the seed data.
